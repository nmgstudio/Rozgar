How to Apply for Samriddh Rozgar-1

Samriddh Rozgar-1 is a semi-government employment initiative by R.R.M.V.R.W. Pvt. Ltd. Interested candidates can apply for positions like Office Assistant, Customer Executive, Data Collector, and Telecaller.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Minimum qualification: 10th Pass / 12th Pass / Graduate
  • Open to all Indian citizens
  • Interview is mandatory after form submission

Application Instructions:

Please read the official notification carefully. Then proceed to register and fill the application form online. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted via WhatsApp or Email (not phone calls).
