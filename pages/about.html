About Us

Company Profile

RRMV Regular Wear Pvt. Ltd., founded on 19th April 2023, is a registered company under Indian corporate laws based in Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. We are engaged in the production and direct marketing of garments and accessories. Our brand name “R.R.” stands for Realynn Rise — a symbol of real fashion rising from the heart of India. With PAN, GST, CIN and ISO 9001:2015 certifications, our operations reflect professionalism and credibility. Led by Respected Ravindra Ray, the company aims to serve affordable fashion to the masses and create employment opportunities at grassroots.

Our Vision & Mission

Our mission is to deliver style with purpose. At Realynn Rise, we believe fashion should not be a privilege of the few but a right for all. We strive to blend Indian aesthetics with global trends and ensure that every citizen, especially youth and small-town aspirants, gets access to decent wearables. Our long-term vision is to generate sustainable employment through fashion and expand into self-reliant manufacturing and digital fashion retail. Each garment we deliver is stitched with dignity, creativity, and opportunity.

Why Fashion Matters

A global survey by Harvard University (2009–2011) revealed that the fashion industry was worth 2.3 trillion USD. Within six years (by 2017–18), it surged to 18.2 trillion USD, reflecting fashion’s massive impact on global economy. Fashion is not just aesthetic — it's a livelihood, a personality enhancer, and a self-expression tool. In countries like India, fashion drives small businesses, artisans, tailors, influencers, and local markets. Fashion empowers individuality and drives confidence in both urban and rural populations alike.

Key Fashion Insights

  • Fashion begins at birth: From the moment a baby is wrapped in cloth, fashion silently becomes part of human life. It evolves with age, status, culture, and profession — proving it is not a luxury but a lifelong presence.
  • Fashion is a basic need: In today’s world, wearing appropriate and stylish clothing isn't only about appearance, it's tied to self-respect, professional presentation, and cultural identity. It shapes first impressions in jobs, interviews, and society.
  • Fashion grows every day: From trending colors to shifting cuts, fashion is a dynamic art. The rapid rise of social media, e-commerce, and influencers proves how quickly styles adapt and influence people daily.
  • Fashion never dies: Trends may fade, but fashion always comes back in cycles — whether it’s bell bottoms from the 70s or vintage kurtas. It renews itself through creativity, making it a sustainable industry.
  • Fashion is reusable: With proper care, clothes can be passed down, upcycled, or styled differently. Realynn Rise promotes garments that last longer, reducing environmental waste and supporting slow fashion.
  • Fashion reflects confidence: What we wear directly affects how we feel. A good outfit can uplift our mood, boost self-esteem, and encourage participation in social and professional spaces.
  • Fashion generates employment: From fabric suppliers to fashion designers, tailors to delivery partners — the fashion industry supports millions of jobs. It is one of the most inclusive industries offering low-entry to high-skill opportunities.
