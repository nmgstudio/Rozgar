About Garment Textile Pvt Ltd

Garment Textile Pvt Ltd is a leading private textile and garment company, renowned for delivering high-quality fashion products and training opportunities. Our mission is to empower local talent and support the textile industry through ethical practices, innovation, and sustainable growth.

  • Established: 2012
  • Headquarters: Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh
  • Specialization: Garment manufacturing, textile design, skill training

We are committed to professional excellence, equal opportunity, and community development.
